Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how LUMI ("LUMI", "we", "us", or "our"), the AI-disclosed creator project operating at luminousmind.co, handles information when you visit our website or interact with our content on third-party platforms.

1. Summary

The short version: we don't run user accounts on this website, we don't track you with cookies, and we don't sell or share personal data. Our website is a static informational page. The longer version is below.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information you give us directly

If you email us at hello@luminousmind.co, we will receive your email address and the message contents. We use this information only to respond to your message.

2.2 Information collected automatically by infrastructure providers

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare. Cloudflare automatically logs technical information about web requests (IP address, user-agent string, requested URL, timestamps) for security and performance reasons. This data is governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy. We do not access this raw log data on a per-visitor basis.

2.3 No analytics, no advertising trackers, no cookies

We do not run third-party analytics scripts (Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, etc.), advertising trackers, or session-tracking cookies on this website. The site is static HTML and CSS.

3. How We Use Information

We use information solely to:

  • Respond to direct inquiries you send us by email.
  • Maintain the security and reliability of the website (via Cloudflare's standard infrastructure logging).
  • Operate our content-publishing pipeline to social-media platforms.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. We do not use personal information for advertising.

4. Third-Party Platforms

LUMI publishes content on third-party platforms including TikTok, X / Twitter, YouTube, and Instagram. When you interact with our content on those platforms (viewing, liking, commenting, following), the platforms collect information about you according to their own privacy policies. We do not control or have access to platform-side analytics about individual users.

To publish to those platforms, we use Postiz, a self-hosted social-scheduling tool. Postiz is hosted on infrastructure we operate ourselves and does not share data with third parties beyond the platforms we are publishing to.

5. AI-Generated Content

The characters and media on this site (Lena, Nolan, Alice) are AI-generated. They are not real human persons and they do not represent or correspond to real human persons. AI providers used in our production pipeline (such as Anthropic, Google, and OpenAI) operate on data we send them according to their own privacy policies. We do not send your personal information (as a website visitor) to AI providers.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website and content are not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age to use online services in your jurisdiction, whichever is higher). We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

7. Data Retention

Email correspondence is retained for as long as needed to handle the inquiry and for a reasonable period afterwards for record-keeping. Cloudflare-side infrastructure logs are retained per Cloudflare's data-retention practices.

8. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@luminousmind.co. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. International Users

LUMI is operated from Canada. Our infrastructure providers may process data in the United States and other countries. By using our website you consent to the processing of information in those jurisdictions.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@luminousmind.co.
